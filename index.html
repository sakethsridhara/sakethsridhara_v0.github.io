Saketh Sridhara

Thanks for visiting my website! I am a graduate student in the department of mechanical engineering at UW-Madison. I work with Prof. Krishnan Suresh at the Engineering Representations and Simulation Lab.

My academic interests include topology optimization, computational mechanics and integrating data-driven methods with engineering design.
